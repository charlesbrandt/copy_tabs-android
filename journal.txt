*2014.06.20 14:27:55 detailed description
Copy Tabs will take the address and title for each tab and create a text list of those.  Firefox for Android does not yet support interacting with the clipboard from an extension, so the contents are posted to a text box in a new tab.  From there, you can copy the list of open links to the clipboard and paste it to your notes. 

In the future, it may make sense to enable pasting and opening a previously stored list, similar to Copy All Urls.
